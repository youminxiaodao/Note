## 下载

https://flywaydb.org/download

PS:本文档演示win版

## 安装

### 解压flyway安装包

### 进入到conf文件夹，修改flyway.conf
#### 1.修改flyway.url
flyway.url=jdbc:sqlserver://localhost:1433;database=your DB
#### 2.修改flyway.user
flyway.user=your username
#### 3.修改flyway.password
flyway.password=your passwd
